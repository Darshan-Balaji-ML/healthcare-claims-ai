Common denial patterns:
Medical necessity denial: ICD and CPT codes are clinically inconsistent.
Duplicate claim denial: same patient, provider, CPT, and date submitted more than once.
Timely filing denial: claim submitted after the payer deadlines (typically 90-365 days).
Prior authorization denial: procedure required advance approval that was not obtained.
